About us

Organic Campus is an NGO that aims to make seasonal, local, organic vegetables and products available to students and others on Science Park. Every two weeks, people can order a surprise bag of vegetables for €10 and a range of extras such as eggs, cheese and mushrooms. Every bag contains one or two lesser known vegetables and recipes to introduce people to a wider variety of seasonal produce. We also host and cook a dinner with vegetables in the spotlight twice a year and a farmer’s market every spring.To make all of this possible we ask regular customers to help us host a pickup or volunteer during one of the bigger events.
